mashed potatoes with beetroot

description

bloody hell potatoes, can be added with spring onion or parsley

ingredients

for 4 servings

  • 400g beetroot
  • 200g potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons of rapeseed oil
  • 2 onions
  • spring onion or parsley (can be dried)
  • 50g butter
  • salt

steps

  1. we will cook beets and potatoes.
  2. in the meantime, finely fry the chopped onion in a teaspoon of rapeseed oil.
  3. finely grate the boiled beetroot.
  4. mash the potatoes and add beetroot with onion and spring onion or parsley to them.
  5. salt the whole mixture and add butter.
